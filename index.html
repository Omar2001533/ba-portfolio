Case 01 — Sales & Profitability

EXCEL · TABLEAU
PRJ-01 / RETAIL-SALES

The profit leak wasn't discounting.

3 / 4 regions losing money on Tables

Diagnosed the single unprofitable product line in a national retail dataset. Discount rates were consistent across every region — ruling out pricing as the cause — which pointed instead to a structural, cost-to-serve problem outside the West region. Delivered as an interactive dashboard plus a one-page executive brief with three prioritized recommendations.

Case 02 — Customer & Delivery Analysis

↳ debugged a silent date-format bug before trusting the result
PRJ-02 / RETENTION-SQL

96.9% of customers never come back.

3.74 vs 4.17 review score, late vs on-time

Queried 96,000+ customers across 9 relational tables in a real e-commerce dataset. Found a 3.1% repeat-purchase rate and a measurable satisfaction gap tied to late delivery — then traced it to one high-volume region carrying a disproportionate share of delays, making it the clearest operational priority rather than the smaller state with the highest raw rate.

Case 03 — Process & Requirements

BPMN · BRD
PRJ-03 / FULFILLMENT-BRD

Three root causes, one requirements doc.

2 days avg. delay from manual picking alone

Mapped an order-fulfillment process end to end, ran simulated stakeholder discovery, and isolated three compounding delays: slow payment validation, batch (not real-time) stock checks, and fully manual picking. Produced current- and future-state process maps plus a full BRD — functional requirements, success metrics, and user stories included.
